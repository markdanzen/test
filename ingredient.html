Classic Lasagna Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans (6 ounces each) tomato paste
  • 2 cans (6 ounces each) tomato sauce
  • 2/3 cup water
  • 2 teaspoons sugar
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil leaves
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups ricotta or cottage cheese
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • 12 cooked lasagna noodles, drained

Instructions:

  1. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef, onion, and garlic over medium heat until the meat is browned. Drain excess fat.
  2. Add crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, tomato sauce, water, sugar, basil, and salt. Simmer for about 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. In a bowl, combine ricotta or cottage cheese, 2 cups mozzarella cheese, Parmesan cheese, and parsley.
  4. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  5. In a 9x13 inch baking dish, layer the lasagna as follows: spread 1 cup of meat sauce, arrange 3 noodles, spread 1/3 of the cheese mixture, and repeat the layers.
  6. Top with remaining mozzarella cheese and Parmesan cheese.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 45 minutes. Let it stand for 15 minutes before serving.
